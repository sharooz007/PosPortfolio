About

I'm Sharooz, based in Kerala, India. Design has been a constant since childhood, starting with graphic design, working through Adobe Illustrator, and eventually taking on logo and branding work for local clients. That foundation shapes everything I build.

Over time that design instinct extended into product and UI work. I care about how things look, how they feel to use, and how the two hold together. The projects here reflect that: each one started with a design problem before it became a technical one.

Alongside the design work, I build what I design. I've been working on two production POS systems and several client websites, working across the full scope from architecture to final UI. Currently finishing my MCA at Sikkim Manipal University.

Spice & Grocery POS

Spice Shop POS

An offline-first desktop POS built on Electron, purpose-built for a spice and grocery retail operation. Handles both retail and wholesale billing modes, full inventory management, and barcode label printing — all without needing internet. A Supabase mirror syncs up when online for owner reporting.

Electron SQLite Tailwind v4 Offline-first Barcode Print Supabase
Active iteration

The working layer is complete — billing, inventory, wholesale/retail modes, barcode printing, offline sync. Three prototype packages have been shipped to the client for testing. Workflow and feature set are still being finalized through client feedback, so UI polish is intentionally held until the technical layer is locked. No point polishing what might still move.
